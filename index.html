Welcome to Teacher's Pet!

Empower your teaching with instant classroom insights! This intuitive app provides teachers with clear, concise summaries of key classroom statistics. Effortlessly aggregate data across classes and time periods, and transform raw numbers into impactful visualizations. Built with a robust database for secure storage and easy access, this app helps you understand student performance trends, identify areas for improvement, and make data-driven decisions to foster student success.
